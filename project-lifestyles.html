THE LIFESTYLES OF FUNGI
Format: 7.5x11" reference book 2019

The Lifestyles of Fungi is a reference book, with original content from an earlier edition of The Encyclopedia of Mushrooms. Additional content was authored and borrowed from other publications, the most notable of which from the book Breaking Open the Head: The Psychedelic Journey to the Heart, by Daniel Pinchbeck.
